About the band

Songs & grooves from Virginia

Soul Strangerz is a Richmond, VA band built around songwriter and guitarist/vocalist Paul Watson, with L.A. Moore on drums and percussion and Michael “Mike” York on guitar and studio work.

Formed in 2024, the band leans into rock, soul, and funky blues, with a little twang and a lot of groove — light-hearted and dark music pulled from real human experience.

Richmond, Virginia Rock / Soul / Funky Blues Groove-heavy originals

Members

  • Paul Watson – vocals, guitar, writer
  • L.A. Moore – drums, percussion
  • Michael E. York – guitar, bass, producer

Songs & grooves written mostly in Virginia and tracked in the studio with the Strangerz crew.
